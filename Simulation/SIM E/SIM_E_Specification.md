# SIM E — Multi-Agent Insurance Market

## Purpose
SIM E introduces a shared-risk insurance market environment.

## Institutional Layer Introduced
Market participation between institutions.

## Model Structure

- multiple underwriting agents
- shared voyage risks
- proportional participation

## Key Mechanisms

- capacity-based line allocation
- shared premium and loss distribution
- capital feedback loops

## Simulation Environment

- multi-agent insurance market
- stochastic loss environment

## Outcome Metrics

- market participation rates
- capacity allocation
- market concentration

## Relationship to Previous Simulation

SIM E extends SIM D by introducing a cooperative insurance market.

## Notes

Loss events occur once per voyage and are distributed across
participating institutions.

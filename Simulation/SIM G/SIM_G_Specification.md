SIM G explores how alternative capital regimes influence underwriting
capacity and market structure.

- expanded capital allocation rules

## Key Mechanisms

- capital expansion
- underwriting capacity scaling

## Simulation Environment

- multi-agent insurance market
- varying capital regimes

## Outcome Metrics

- market concentration
- underwriting capacity growth
- survival rates

## Relationship to Previous Simulation

SIM G extends SIM F by experimenting with alternative capital rules.

# SIM F — Market Coordination Model

## Purpose
SIM F introduces market coordination mechanisms governing the renewal
and continuation of cooperative underwriting arrangements.

## Institutional Layer Introduced
Market coordination.

## Model Structure

- multiple underwriting institutions
- shared voyage risks
- coordinated renewal decisions

## Key Mechanisms

- owner renewal decisions
- tenure-based line allocation
- institutional trust collapse states

## Simulation Environment

- multi-agent market
- stochastic risk environment

## Outcome Metrics

- institutional survival
- market share dynamics
- trust collapse frequency

## Relationship to Previous Simulation

SIM F extends SIM E by introducing coordination mechanisms governing
market participation.

## Notes

The model captures coordination dynamics within cooperative insurance
markets.

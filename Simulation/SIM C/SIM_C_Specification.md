# SIM C — Core Financial Engine

## Purpose
SIM C introduces the financial structure of underwriting institutions.

The simulation examines how capital constraints influence underwriting
behaviour across time.

## Institutional Layer Introduced
Firm-level financial structure.

## Model Structure

- underwriting agents
- capital resources
- retained profits
- stochastic loss events

## Key Mechanisms

- capital allocation to underwriting
- profit retention
- investment income on capital

## Simulation Environment

- multiple underwriting agents
- repeated underwriting cycles

## Outcome Metrics

- capital evolution
- survival probability
- profit accumulation

## Relationship to Previous Simulation

SIM C extends SIM B by introducing capital and financial constraints.

## Notes

The simulation begins to approximate simplified institutional
underwriting behaviour.
